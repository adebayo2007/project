    Our K9 security teams are made up of highly skilled handlers and large canine breeds that are frequently used as police and guard dogs, such as German shepherds, Doberman pinschers, Rottweilers, and others. These dogs are a great deterrence against crime because they are impressive and terrifying. Our K9 security dogs have also been taught to detect unusual sounds or activities and to notify their masters to the presence of illegal narcotics, explosives, and hidden weapons.

    Training of dogs to perform as a guard or watch dog requires special skills only available to security intelligence professionals. A properly trained security dog can effectively replace up to 3 static security officers. Through expertise and experience we provide the best k9 services in the country. Our dogs have an incredible sense of hearing and a powerful sense of smell that can enable them to detect disturbances from a long distance, well before any person or high tech alarm will respond.

    Our K9 Services include:

    • Supply of Security Dogs
    • Training of Security Dogs
    • Handling of Security Dogs
    • Deployment of Security Dogs for Day and Night duties

    SUPPLY OF SECURITY DOGS

    We supply trained security dogs to individuals and organisations. Our security dogs consist of; Guard Dogs: This species of dogs are used to guard against intruders and watch for unwanted or unexpected people or animals. They are generally not trained to attack but to alert owners of a stranger’s presence. Watch or Attack Dogs: These kinds of dogs have been trained to defend or attack on the owner’s command. They will not attack unless their owners are facing threat or have commanded an attack. They are the types used as police dogs or used by the military as sentry guards.
